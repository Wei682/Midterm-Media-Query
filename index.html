Chinese Folk Art

Chinese folk art has a rich history, and many traditions are still practiced today. There are numerous kinds of folk art, but unfortunately, most of them are not familiar to the world. It's time to explore the essence of Chinese creativity and learn more about the cultural heritages!

From the mesmerizing performances of shadow puppetry to the intricate designs of silk embroidery, traditional Chinese arts and crafts encompass various forms and techniques.
